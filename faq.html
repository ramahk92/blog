Frequently Asked Questions

This portal supports complaint categories: Library, Lab, Hostel, Wi-Fi, and Other.

How can students raise a complaint?

Register as student, login, and use the complaint submission form in the student dashboard.

What is the complaint workflow?

Submitted → In Review → In Progress → Resolved / Rejected.

How are complaints prioritized?

Complaints can be marked as Low, Medium, or High priority. Priority affects expected resolution timeline.

How does escalation work?

If expected resolution time is crossed while complaint is open, the system marks it escalated and notifies student/admin.

Attachment and safety rules

Only PNG/JPG/WEBP/PDF files up to 2MB are accepted. Inputs are sanitized to reduce injection risk.
